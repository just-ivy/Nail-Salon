Booking & Cancellation

  • Our services are primarily by appointment, with walk-ins accommodated only if space permits.
  • Cancellations can be done up to 24 hours in advance.
  • Please arrive at least 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment.
  • A 10-minute grace period is allowed, after which service is not guaranteed.
  • Children under 6 years of age will not be attended to.
  • Kids are classified as children between ages 6-12.

Tips and Gifts

  • We recognize that tips and gifts are an expression of gratitude for satisfaction

  • Non-cash gifts may be given directly to the employee or handed to the manager on duty with the employee's name.
